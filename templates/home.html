Understanding the Fire Weather Index (FWI)

FWI Scale

The Fire Weather Index ranges from 0 to 100+. Values below 5 indicate low danger, 5-15 moderate, 15-30 high, and above 30 indicates extreme fire danger.

Ridge Regression

Our model uses Ridge Regression, a regularized linear regression technique that prevents overfitting and provides stable predictions for fire weather conditions.

Algerian Dataset

Trained on real forest fire data from two Algerian regions (Bejaia & Sidi Bel-abbes) collected during June to September fire season.
